User Data Deletion

Effective Date: December 2025

Bloggle is a student project created for educational purposes.

If you logged into Bloggle using Google or Facebook and want your data removed, you can request deletion at any time.

What data is deleted

Upon request, we will permanently delete:

  • Your user account
  • Your name and email address
  • Any associated OAuth identifiers
  • Your content (posts, comments, votes)

How to request deletion

Email us at:

anesmehovic28@gmail.com

Include:

  • The email address you used to log in
  • The provider you used (Google or Facebook)

Processing time

Requests are processed manually. Your deletion request will be completed within 7 days.

Confirmation

Once your data has been deleted, we will send you a confirmation email.
